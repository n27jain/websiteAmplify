Lying Lawyer

Born in a strict household, Vijay a fun loving mathematical genius is forced into becoming a lawyer. But rather than trying to make as much money as possible like his father pushes, he decides to take virtually no compensation from his clients. All he asks from his clients is a single favor for anything that he wants in the future. Because favors cannot be legally binding to anyone, all of his clients agree. But what does he want? What are his true intentions? How is it that he is able to defend clients in a way that is almost clairvoyance? In this twisted game of justice, who will be victorious, who snuffed out? Good and bad is never black and white, but are those who win justice, or those who follow the path of justice victorious? We all have secrets that can condemn us in the appropriate society, but what if one makes their own society? Hobby Screenplay.
